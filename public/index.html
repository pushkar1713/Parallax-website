My favourite stories

Hello, My name is Pushkar and I am just an avid consumer of popular storytelling mediums like novels, films, manga, anime, tv shows and graphic novels.

Stories can also evoke feelings within us, whether we know the individual or not. Good stories can make us feel excitement, anger, sadness, empathy, or enthusiasm. Our emotional reaction can mean we feel something towards the person telling the story, which helps create connection.

MR. ROBOT

"Mr. Robot" follows Elliot (Rami Malek), a young programmer who works as a cyber-security engineer by day and a vigilante hacker by night. Elliot finds himself at a crossroads when the mysterious leader (Christian Slater) of an underground hacker group recruits him to destroy the corporation he is paid to protect.

As a Computer Science student this was a great experience for me, Rami Malek totally nailed the role of Elliot and how the plot develops and progress with each and every single episode is masterful love this series very much.

12 ANGRY MEN

12 Angry Men is a 1957 film about a jury of 12 men who deliberate the fate of a teenager accused of murder. The jurors must put aside their personal conflicts to reach a verdict. The case involves a teenager accused of stabbing his father to death. If convicted, the boy will be sentenced to death.

This is probably the most thrilling 97 mins I have experienced while watching a movie. Each and every dialouge and performances by the actors is just phenomenal.
